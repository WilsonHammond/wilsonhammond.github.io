Governance, Risk, and Compliance Lab 2

CivicCare Nexus Healthcare, Finance, and Federal Risk Lab

This fictional Governance, Risk, and Compliance lab examines a health technology company that handles patient data, consumer financial information, and federal program records. The project turns one cloud storage near miss into a full risk analysis involving data classification, control mapping, evidence requests, corrective action, and executive reporting.

Project Objective

The goal of this lab was to practice how a Governance, Risk, and Compliance analyst identifies sensitive data, maps obligations to control expectations, requests evidence, documents control gaps, recommends remediation, and explains residual risk to leadership.

Scenario

CivicCare Nexus is a fictional health technology company with 3 business lines: healthcare services, patient finance services, and federal program support. A cloud storage location was found with overly broad access. The records were synthetic in this lab, but the weakness would matter in the real world because the production environment handles electronic protected health information, consumer financial information, sensitive personally identifiable information, and Controlled Unclassified Information.

Framework and Compliance Lens

  • Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act Security Rule
  • Gramm Leach Bliley Act Safeguards Rule
  • National Institute of Standards and Technology Cybersecurity Framework 2.0
  • National Institute of Standards and Technology Special Publication 800 53
  • National Institute of Standards and Technology Special Publication 800 171
  • Federal Risk and Authorization Management Program cloud authorization concepts
  • Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard 4.0.1 payment security reference

Featured Deliverables

  • Risk register with likelihood, impact, inherent risk, residual risk, treatment, owner, and evidence needed
  • Control crosswalk connecting risk areas to National Institute of Standards and Technology control families and sector obligations
  • Evidence request list for identity, data protection, logging, vendor risk, incident response, federal data handling, and recovery
  • Plan of Action and Milestones for corrective action tracking
  • Executive summary and interview talk track
