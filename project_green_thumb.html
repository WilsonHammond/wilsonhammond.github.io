Governance, Risk, and Compliance Lab

Small Business Cyber Risk Assessment and Risk Register

I completed a Governance, Risk, and Compliance case study for a fictional small business nursery that depends on cloud based point of sale operations, customer data collection, living inventory records, supplier availability, physical security monitoring, water access, and employee safety controls.

Project Objective

The purpose of the project was to review business interview evidence and convert it into a structured risk register that leadership could use to prioritize treatment.

Featured Findings

The technology risk showed how a cloud based point of sale disruption could affect customer transactions, personally identifiable information, sales reporting, inventory updates, and business continuity at the same time.

The asset management risk showed how living inventory tracked across 2 systems can create record drift when sales, terminations, disease losses, or care updates are not reconciled.
